# FieldOps IQ Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

FieldOps IQ is designed for telecom field technicians to create job notes, fault reports, voice notes, equipment observations, site surveys, work packs, PDF reports, and subscription state.

## Data Collection

FieldOps IQ does not collect personal data through a developer-operated server, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, or tracking system in the current App Store build.

Information entered into the app, including customer names, job locations, service notes, photos, voice transcripts, survey notes, equipment observations, generated reports, and subscription state, is stored locally on the user's device using Apple's local storage frameworks.

## Device Permissions

FieldOps IQ may request access to device features so technicians can use app functionality:

- Camera and photo library access are used to attach equipment, job, and site survey evidence.
- Microphone and speech recognition access are used to capture and transcribe technician voice notes.
- Notifications may be used for optional job or fault reminders.
- StoreKit is used for Apple-managed in-app subscriptions.

These permissions are used to provide app features. FieldOps IQ does not use them for advertising or cross-app tracking.

## Third-Party Services

The current App Store build uses Apple's platform services, including StoreKit and system speech recognition permissions. Payment processing and subscription management are handled by Apple.

FieldOps IQ does not include third-party advertising, third-party analytics, or third-party tracking SDKs in the current App Store build.

## User Control

Users can delete locally stored app content from within the app where deletion controls are provided, or remove app data by deleting the app from the device. Device permissions can be changed at any time in iOS Settings.

## Changes

If FieldOps IQ adds a developer-operated backend, remote AI processing, analytics, account features, or other data collection in a future version, this policy and the App Store privacy information will be updated before release.
